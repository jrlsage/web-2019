Hi, I'm Adam, and I make products better through people!

When I build them, I focus on the entirety of the experience, specifically, "who it is for" and "what it is for." This point of view means I opt for the following:

  • Story over Features
  • Value over Speed
  • Research over Ego

Generally, this means wrestling with my fears of being enough and focusing squarely on the other person, leading conversations with "I don't know." I firmly believe that my ability to build comes from dancing with my fear of the unknown and leading with my curiosity once I do.
